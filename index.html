Welcome!

This project focused on the usage of PrEP amongst UCLA students and their opinions on its accessibility. To view where current PrEP users access their PrEP, click on the interactive pie chart on the left to be shown on our map along with each individual's story. Click on a user's story to immediately be taken to the location of where PrEP is provided to them and learn more about their opinions on PrEP accessibility at UCLA.

If you would like to see what UCLA students who do not take PrEP currently think of PrEP's accessibility at UCLA, click on the red portion of our pie chart to be taken to a general view of UCLA and read their accounts. Scroll down to read each individual's own opinion on accessibility of PrEP here at UCLA.

Click the button below to move on and see the map, and feel free to refer to the instructions again by clicking the button above our mapplication to see this again.

Thank you for viewing, and if you would like to share your own story, please consider taking our survey!
